Stage 1: The Mouth 👅

Stage 1/5 - 20% Complete

Food is chewed inside the mouth and mixed with saliva + salivary amylase.

What's Happening in Your Mouth? 🤔

Mechanical Digestion

Your teeth chew food into smaller pieces. This is called mechanical digestion.

Chemical Digestion Begins

Saliva mixes with food. It contains salivary amylase, which starts breaking down starch.

💡 Fun Facts About Your Mouth:

  • You produce about 1-2 liters of saliva every day.
  • Saliva helps make food soft and easy to swallow.
  • The chewed food ball is called a bolus.

📖 Key Terms:

Bolus: Chewed food mixed with saliva.
Enzyme: A protein that helps chemical reactions.
Salivary Amylase: Enzyme in saliva that breaks down starch.
